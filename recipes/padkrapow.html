Pad Kra Pow

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¾ teaspoon salt, divided
  1. On a plate, combine flour, cumin, cayenne pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Stir to distribute the spices. Coat pork chops with flour mixture, and shake off any excess.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place pork chops in the hot skillet, and fry for about 4 minutes per side, until cooked through.
